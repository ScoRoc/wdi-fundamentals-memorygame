Instructions

Move your mouse around the screen to generate a fading color trail. If you like a color CLICK it to stick it! If you no longer want that color, just CLICK it again. If you want to generate a random color board, hit the GENERATE RANDOM BOARD button. To reset to a blank board, hit RESET BOARD .
